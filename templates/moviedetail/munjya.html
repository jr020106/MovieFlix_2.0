Munjya

Year: 2024 | Rating: 6.6★| Duration: 2h 3min

In the heart of a small village, a young man's peaceful visit to his ancestral home takes a terrifying turn when he unknowingly unleashes a restless spirit — Munjya. As ancient folklore collides with modern life, he must uncover long-buried secrets to end the haunting. Blending spine-chilling horror with unexpected comedy, "Munjya" takes viewers on a thrilling yet emotional journey through tradition, fear, and redemption.

Genres: Horror, Comedy, Folklore, Supernatural Thriller

Director: Aditya Sarpotdar

Writers: Yogesh Chandekar, Niren Bhatt

Epic action movie with an overdose of twists and turns
The action scenes are spectacular and vary from explosive car chases to combats in an old church that has fallen into disrepair. The special effects have been designed with care as the camera work and light techniques are very solid except from one unnecessarily nauseating scene in a conference room. The beautiful locations and settings are very colourful and vary from India over Italy to the Arctic Circle.

Bollywood has now Raised the Bar in Action!
A well executed piece of Action art that seriously surpassed my expectations! Considering the budget and the technology Bollywood have in hands ; this surely set the standards few notches up. The casting of Hrithik and Tiger made it much appealing too! They duly justified their respective roles. Locations, Cinematography, CGI all looked pretty good considering it's Bollywood. It's still not close of Hollywood regarding the Action pieces but I must admit it surprised me a lot with what it already had.
